# ⚔️ Dark Souls CRUD Web Service 🔥💀

> *"YOU DIED... but your data persisted."*

Welcome to the land of Lordran 🏰 — where death is a feature, not a bug, and every database record is one bad decision away from being soft-locked forever. Tread carefully, Undead. The SQL won't forgive you twice.

---

## 🌑 What Is This Cursed Thing?

This web application is a full-stack CRUD interface themed around the brutally unforgiving world of **Dark Souls**. Built by **Ben & Jared** ✨ (Team 200), it lets you conjure, inspect, mutate, and banish the very entities that make Lordran tick:

- 🧟 **Characters** — The haggard Undead who refuse to stay hollowed. Give them a name, a class, a level, and send them to their doom.
- 🛡️ **Factions** — Shadowy covenants and desperate alliances vying for the age of fire. Define their alignment, their leader, and the territory they bleed for.
- 🗡️ **Weapons** — The devastating instruments of suffering used to settle every grudge in the most punishing ways imaginable. Every build needs a weapon. Choose wisely.

---

## 🔮 How Does It Work?

On the front end 🖥️, a dark, **particle-drenched** HTML/CSS/JavaScript client serves as your bonfire — a glowing hub where you fill out forms and fire off fetch requests like throwing embers into the abyss. ✨ The UI is as moody as Blighttown and twice as dangerous to your sanity.

On the back end ⚙️, a **PHP REST API** stands guard like a fog-gate, routing your **Create, Read, Update, and Delete** requests through to a MySQL database 🗄️ that holds all the lore of Lordran. Cross the threshold and your data either makes it through... or it doesn't.

---

## 🎮 What Can You Do?

| Action | What Happens |
|--------|-------------|
| 📖 **READ** | Peer into the database like gazing into the Abyss |
| ➕ **INSERT** | Summon a new record into existence |
| ✏️ **UPDATE** | Rewrite fate — tweak a warrior's stats mid-campaign |
| 🗑️ **DELETE** | Purge. Erase. Send them back to the bonfire permanently. |
